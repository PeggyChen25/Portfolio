Enchantments Lottery 2024

Objective

The goal of this project was to analyze the 2024 Enchantments Lottery permit data to help applicants improve their chances of securing a coveted backpacking permit, using data insights to recommend the best application strategies.

Problem

With only 6.41% of applications accepted in 2024, the lottery faced overwhelming demand and extremely low success rates. Applicants lacked data-driven guidance on which choices, zones, group sizes, or dates would improve their chances of winning a permit.

Action

  • Data analysis: Examined acceptance rates by choice rank, group size, zone, and week, revealing that first-choice selections, smaller group sizes (especially solo), and the Eightmile/Caroline zone had the highest success rates.
  • Trend analysis: Investigated success rate trends over weeks, showing that fall dates had higher success rates due to lower competition, and analyzed weather patterns from 1981‚Äì2024 to recommend optimal hiking windows.
  • Recommendations: Identified three top permit dates (June 8, June 23, June 30) with ideal weather (over 52¬∞F, <0.03 inches rainfall) and provided group size recommendations for each.
  • Visualization: Created clear charts and visuals to communicate selection patterns and recommendations, enhancing understanding for future applicants.

Result

Delivered a set of actionable recommendations that increased applicants' understanding of their odds and improved strategy, helping them choose the best dates, zones, and group sizes to maximize success in the next lottery cycle.

Why this matters

This project empowered outdoor enthusiasts with data-driven insights, transforming an opaque lottery process into a more transparent and navigable experience. By blending data storytelling with practical recommendations, we made it easier for people to access and enjoy one of the Pacific Northwest‚Äôs most iconic wilderness destinations.
