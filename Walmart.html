Walmart Sales Forecasting

Objective

Develop a comprehensive forecasting model for Walmart's weekly sales by evaluating and comparing time series models, panel data models, and machine learning approaches to enhance inventory management and strategic planning.

Problem

Walmart's weekly sales data exhibit fluctuations influenced by seasonality, store-specific attributes, macroeconomic factors, and consumer behavior patterns. Traditional forecasting methods struggled to capture these complexities, leading to inventory inefficiencies and missed revenue opportunities.

Action

  • Time Series Modeling: Implemented SARIMA models to capture seasonal patterns and historical dependencies in sales data. Stationarity was assessed using the Augmented Dickey-Fuller test, and model parameters were optimized based on ACF and PACF analyses.
  • Panel Data Modeling: Applied Fixed Effects, Random Effects, and Mundlak models to account for both within-store and between-store variations, incorporating interaction terms to capture combined effects of macroeconomic variables like CPI and unemployment rates.
  • Machine Learning Approaches: Utilized Random Forest and Boosted Trees algorithms to model complex, non-linear relationships in the data, enhancing predictive accuracy.
  • Model Validation: Compared models using metrics such as RMSE and MAPE to evaluate forecasting accuracy and determine the most effective approach.
  • Visualization: Created interactive dashboards to visualize sales trends, model predictions, and key insights, facilitating stakeholder understanding and decision-making.

Result

The SARIMA model effectively captured seasonal sales patterns, while panel data models provided insights into store-specific factors influencing sales. Machine learning models, particularly Boosted Trees, achieved higher predictive accuracy. The integrated approach enabled Walmart to better anticipate sales fluctuations, optimize inventory levels, and improve overall operational efficiency.

Why this matters

Accurate sales forecasting is critical for large retailers like Walmart to manage inventory, plan promotions, and meet customer demand. By leveraging a combination of statistical and machine learning models, this project demonstrated a robust framework for capturing complex sales dynamics, leading to more informed business strategies and enhanced profitability.
