Brooks Running Forecasting Demand 2027

For this project, we’re partnering with Brooks Running to analyze sales data and explore how product and color attributes influence sales performance. New York Hospitals Efficiency Analysis

The New York State Department of Health invites you to explore SPARCS data — over 1.19 million hospital discharge records from 2009–2024. This Hospital Efficiency Analysis project dives into key metrics like costs, outcomes, and resource use, using visual tools and models to uncover trends, spot inefficiencies, and suggest improvements in healthcare delivery.

Walmart Sales Forecasting

In this project, we analyzed over 421,000 weekly sales records from 45 Walmart stores across 99 departments to uncover key sales drivers, seasonal trends, and improve forecasting accuracy. Using regression, time series analysis, and advanced visualizations, we delivered insights to support Walmart’s data-driven business decisions.

  • More

Enchantments Lottery 2024

The Enchantments project uses Tableau to analyze historical weather data and 2024 lottery results, helping applicants improve their chances of securing a 4-day backpacking permit. By identifying optimal dates based on favorable weather conditions and lower competition, the project highlights specific dates that can boost permit success rates.

  • More

Financial Transaction Analysis

This project analyzes 2M+ financial transactions to uncover client risk profiles, spending patterns, and transaction behaviors across the U.S. It includes three dashboards: (1) Client Segment & Risk, (2) Card Usage & Brand Behavior, and (3) Transaction Behavior. Together, they provide insights to support smarter financial strategies.
